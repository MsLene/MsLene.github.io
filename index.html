About Me

People-first,
data-informed.

I'm an HR professional with over 8 years of experience spanning talent acquisition, organizational development, and HR technology. I've partnered with fast-scaling startups and established enterprises alike to build hiring pipelines that actually work — and cultures that retain the people you fight hard to bring in.

My approach is straightforward: combine structured process with genuine human connection. I believe the best hires come from knowing exactly what a team needs, not just matching keywords on a résumé. And the best cultures are built through intentional design, not luck.

When I'm not optimizing a hiring funnel or facilitating a calibration session, you'll find me mentoring early-career HR practitioners and speaking at local industry meetups.
